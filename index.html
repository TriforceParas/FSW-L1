Complete Platform

What CertiFlow Can Handle

The application is designed to reduce manual work across audit planning, reminders, documents, review and certification activities.

01

Audit Planning

Create audit plans with customer details, audit type, standards, stage dates and assigned auditors.

02

Email Scheduler

Send audit due emails to clients and assignment emails to auditors automatically.

03

Document Generation

Generate audit plan documents without manually preparing the same formats again and again.

04

Time Complexity Calculation

Calculate audit effort and timing to create better audit plans based on audit requirements.

05

AFR Processing

Validate uploaded Audit Finding Reports and generate further documents and presentations.

06

Certificates and Reviews

Track reviews, delayed audits, upcoming audits and issued certificate records in one place.

Detailed Process Flow

Planning Stage

Admin adds customer details, selects ISO standard, calculates required audit time and prepares the audit schedule.

Execution Stage

Auditors receive assignment emails, conduct audit activities and upload the Audit Finding Report.

Completion Stage

The system validates AFR data, generates further documents, prepares presentations and stores certificate records.
